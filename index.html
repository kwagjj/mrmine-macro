Welcome to MR.MINE MACRO page

Under the frustration of constantly checking the Mr.Mine tab every one or two minutes, I've decided to make a macro that will do the ridiculously repetitive and boring work for me.

MAIN FEATURES

I have made several versions of the macro of my own each improved compared to the previous one. I am saving and organizing my progress in this github. But the main features of my current progress is as follows:

  • automatic selling
  • automatic looting
  • show record of looting
  • open config windows for these features with a simple hotkey
